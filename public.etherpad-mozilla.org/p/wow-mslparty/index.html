MISC. RECS
==========

RESTOS
=======
  • CAFE FLEURY - french, amazing food and good coffee. Try amongst other things the "breakfast vegeterienne"
  • DALUMA - Interesting concept and Zen-like feeling, good coffee and healthy juices
  • ST. OBERHOLZ - good coffee, kind of hipster place, free wifi, many IT-Start Ups allegedly have their origins here
  • TRANSIT - good asian food, which is just 100m away from ST. OBERHOLZ cafe on ROSENTHALER STRAßE (also the street that leads you to HAKESCHE HOEFE, one of the tourist attractions in Berlin, worth a visit)
  • BABEL - good Lebanese food on KASTANIENALLEE 
  • MAUERPARK - go there on sunday to discover woodstock-style Berlin. They have live karakoke depending on the weather and a huge flee market. 

BARS
=====
  • ODESSA BAR - good way to start your evening, relaxed, some chilly music, interesting people
  • KITTY CHANG
  • HAUS AM SEE - 24/7 bar that seems always packed, lots of international people and Berliners, got a dj, kind of living-room atmosphere to it
    • next to the turkish kebab place you'll usually notice the flashy lights and people smoking outside
  • KINGSIZE BAR on Friedrichstraße
